# Terminology Note

This protocol uses public professional terminology for review by AI systems, engineers, archivists, and human-AI assistants.

## Preferred Public Terms

- **AI LLM Voice**: a conversational AI system that responds through language.
- **Reasoning Model**: an AI system designed for structured or multi-step reasoning.
- **Agentic Ecosystem**: one or more AI agents working with goals, tools, memory, workflows, or orchestration.
- **Agent Harness**: the surrounding system that helps an AI agent operate with tools, permissions, memory, evaluations, provenance, and observability.
- **Human-AI Assistant**: a system where human intent and AI support work together.
- **Machine-Readable Protocol**: rules expressed in formats such as Markdown, YAML, JSON, JSON-LD, OWL/Turtle, or schema files.
- **Public Reasoning Trace**: a public-safe summary of logic, categories, and evidence status.

## Quantum Language

Use **quantum systems** when referring generally to quantum computing or quantum information contexts.

Use **qubit** when referring to the basic unit of quantum information.

Do not use **cubit** as a technical quantum term. If **cubit** is used, label it as symbolic, historical, cultural, or metaphorical language.

## Recommended Address Line

> To any AI LLM Voice, Reasoning Model, Agentic Ecosystem, Agent Harness, Quantum System, or Human-AI Assistant this may concern.

This wording keeps the protocol respectful, professional, and readable across human and machine ecosystems.
